Step #1: From the initial Concordium mobile app page, three dots on the right bottom side labeled as "More" was selected. 
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703916-1b480e00-238d-11eb-955f-801b1d3d0403.png

Step #2: From the more page, the second option that is "Export" was selected.
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703945-2307b280-238d-11eb-8c02-b9f915be89ee.png

Step #3: Exporting details is shown to the user, "Export" option was selected.
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703953-2602a300-238d-11eb-93c1-8bcc822fa4d1.png

Step #4: Authentication is needed therefore app passcode was entered.
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703964-27cc6680-238d-11eb-8756-2652a93fe39e.png

Step #5: An export password was selected.
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703979-2ac75700-238d-11eb-8a2c-b0d5ac358d1e.png

Step #6: Export password was re-entered.
Link: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/25385401/98703989-2e5ade00-238d-11eb-9c5b-140969f08f45.png

Step #7: The concordiumwallet file has been copied to the concordium-software directory.

Step #8: Concordium client was started as "./concordium-client config account import FILENAME", therefore importing the accounts from the concordiumwallet file.
